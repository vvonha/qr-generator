What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information. When scanned with a smartphone camera, it can instantly open websites, display text, connect to WiFi networks, or perform other actions. QR codes are widely used in marketing, payments, and information sharing.
